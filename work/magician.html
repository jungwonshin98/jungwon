Side Project

Magician

Category

Graphic Art

Date

Tuesday, Jan 23, 2018

When I was younger, I always dreamed of becoming a magician - a person with magical power, who can fly, read people's minds, teleport to places, and more. And, as a twenty-years-old, I still believe in magical power. I think that a force - the magical power - helps people when people pursue their dreams. The universe wants us to succeed; even though it will not spoon-feed us to our destinations, when we are determined and take the chances, the force will be on our side.

This is the magician I pictured when I was younger.
