Of the Universe

Jungwon Shin

Side Project

Of the Universe

Category

Print Art

Dimensions

(27 ½ in * 19 ¾ in)

I am standing in the midst of stillness. The stillness surrounds me, submerges my whole body; I am inside the stillness. I no longer know what part of me is or is not part of the universe. Every atom in my body is from a collapsing star. In the expanse of space, atoms were fused and elements formed – I was created. My mind and body all began from the pursing star; I am of the universe.
