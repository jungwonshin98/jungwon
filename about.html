About

My name is Jungwon Shin. I am currently working as Chief Marketing Officer at Quus, a blockchain-based startup company that aims to decentralize the current ecosystem for small businesses and independent writers.

I earned the Master's in Engineering - Computer Science Cornell Tech in 2021 and Bachelor's in Science, majoring in Information Science and minoring in Business in 2020. I want to create technologies that is user-centered, context-aware, and value-sensitive to better serve marginalized communities.

I was born in Seoul, Korea and live in New York City. I love reading books, playing videogames, snacking, cooking, and expanding my horizon, whatever that means. I used to love traveling, but since COVID-19, I've learned to enjoy my time home.
